More About Gubis85
===================

Gubis85 Solutions (Pty) Ltd is a BBBEE accredited Security Company which was established in 2013 by a black serial entrepreneur. Possessing a team with extensive backgrounds and expertise in the sector, Gubis85 has identified the inadequacies in local, regional and national security and protection companies and have seized the opportunity to not only forge sustainable solutions but create employment in South Africa. We are a 100% black owned company.

Over the past 11 years, Gubis85 has solidified its footprint as a significant role player in the security industry. We pride ourselves in providing sophisticated and professional security services. Moreover, we boast over 10 clients who can attest to our satisfactory balance of appropriate and cost-effective surveillance solutions; while continually meeting their ever-changing preferences.

Whilst Gubis85 is based in Gauteng, we have forged beneficial partnerships across all regions and offer a wide range of services across a national and global network. Our satellite offices are situated in Limpopo, North-West, Mpumalanga, Free State and KwaZulu Natal.
